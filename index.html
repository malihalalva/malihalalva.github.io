About Me

Introduction

Hello there! I'm Maliha, currently immersed in the world of Mechanical Engineering at McMaster University in Ontario, Canada.

My passion lies in the vast expanse of space, where I envision myself contributing to groundbreaking research and development endeavors. During my journey as a fourth-year student, I had the privilege of being part of the team that constructed NEUDOSE, McMaster University's pioneering satellite project. This experience ignited my ambition to delve deeper into the realms of space exploration. My ultimate aspiration is to be at the forefront of space research and development, pushing the boundaries of our understanding of the universe.

Eager to embrace challenges and expand my knowledge base, I'm enthusiastic about seizing opportunities to contribute to future space missions. Whether it's through co-op placements or collaborative projects, I'm keen to be a valuable team player in the next chapter of space exploration.
